MISSILE DEFENSE AGENCY 
  
Lockheed Martin Corp., Grand Prairie, Texas, is being awarded a $19,872,914 modification (P00023) to a previously awarded fixed-price contract.  The value of this modification is $19,872,914, increasing the total contract value from $896,669,237 to $916,542,151.  Under this modification, the contractor will produce two additional Lot 8 Interceptors and provide associated production support efforts for the Missile Defense Agency Terminal High Altitude Area Defense Project Office under fixed-price incentive-firm target contract line item numbers.  The work will be performed in Grand Prairie, Texas; Huntsville, Alabama; Anniston, Alabama; Camden, Arkansas; and Troy, Alabama.  The performance period is through Oct. 31, 2019.  Fiscal 2016 procurement funds in the amount of $19,872,914 are being obligated on this award.  The Missile Defense Agency, Huntsville, Alabama, is the contracting activity (# HQ0147-14-C-0011).
